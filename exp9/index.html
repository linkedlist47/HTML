About Our Department

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To advance the frontiers of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science through innovative research, quality education, and meaningful industry collaborations. We strive to create an environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and ethical application of AI technologies.

Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in AI and Data Science education and research, producing leaders who drive technological innovation and solve complex real-world problems through intelligent systems.

Programs Offered

  • B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
  • M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence
  • M.Tech in Data Science
  • Ph.D. in AI and Data Science
  • Executive Programs for Industry Professionals

Research Areas

Our department focuses on cutting-edge research in various domains including Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Robotics, Big Data Analytics, and Ethical AI. We collaborate with industries and research institutions worldwide to address complex challenges.

Our Courses

Comprehensive curriculum covering all aspects of AI and Data Science

Undergraduate Courses

AIDS101 - 4 Credits

Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

Foundational concepts, history, and applications of AI. Covers search algorithms, knowledge representation, and basic problem-solving.

AIDS102 - 4 Credits

Programming for Data Science

Python programming, data structures, and libraries for data analysis including NumPy, Pandas, and visualization tools.

AIDS201 - 4 Credits

Machine Learning Fundamentals

Supervised and unsupervised learning algorithms, model evaluation, and practical implementation of ML solutions.

AIDS202 - 4 Credits

Data Structures and Algorithms

Advanced data structures and algorithm design with applications in AI and data science problems.

AIDS301 - 4 Credits

Deep Learning

Neural networks, backpropagation, CNNs, RNNs, and applications in computer vision and natural language processing.

AIDS302 - 4 Credits

Big Data Analytics

Distributed computing, Hadoop, Spark, and techniques for processing and analyzing large-scale datasets.

Postgraduate Courses

AIDS501 - 4 Credits

Advanced Machine Learning

Ensemble methods, Bayesian learning, reinforcement learning, and advanced optimization techniques.

AIDS502 - 4 Credits

Natural Language Processing

Text processing, sentiment analysis, machine translation, and transformer models for NLP tasks.

AIDS503 - 4 Credits

Computer Vision

Image processing, object detection, segmentation, and deep learning applications in computer vision.

AIDS504 - 4 Credits

Reinforcement Learning

Markov decision processes, Q-learning, policy gradients, and applications in robotics and game AI.
